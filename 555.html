Ayutthaya historical Park

The most famous place which a lot of people visit. The park is designated as UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Wat Mahathat

Buddist temples and a famed Buddha head surrounded by trees. A must-visit place in Ayutthaya.

Wat Phra Sri Sanphet

Ruins of Thailand's ancient temples destroyed in 1767.

Wat Yai Chai Mang Khon

One of the least visited places in Ayutthaya. There is a sleeping Buddha and some temples.
